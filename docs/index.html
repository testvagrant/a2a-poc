How UTA Works

1

Test Agent Initiates

UTA sends an initial message to your AI agent, simulating a real user interaction with context and intent.

2

Real Agent Responds

Your AI agent (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.) responds naturally based on its training and capabilities.

3

Budget Enforcement

System monitors conversation length, latency, and cost to ensure tests stay within defined limits.

4

LLM Judge Evaluation

GPT-4o analyzes the entire conversation for quality, relevance, completeness, and compliance.
